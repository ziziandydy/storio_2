The Vision

Not a database.
A Pensieve.

In Storio, every entry is a memory card — not a row in a spreadsheet. Your folio holds your relationship with each story: when you encountered it, what you felt, and how your perspective shifted when you returned years later.

The Story Behind

From Data PM to Build PM.

Storio was built entirely through pure vibe coding and AI tools. Prior to this project, I had zero coding experience.

I am currently transitioning from a Data PM to a Build PM. This journey represents a holistic approach to building software—where I drove everything from product definition and UX decisions to the actual full-stack implementation.

It's a proof of concept that with modern AI tools, product visionaries can bring their complete ideas to life without being blocked by traditional engineering constraints.
