## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Team Profile Display
The landing page SHALL have a section dedicated to the 5 core team members, displaying their portraits and titles.

#### Scenario: Viewing Team Members
- **WHEN** the user scrolls to the Team section
- **THEN** they SHALL see the following members:
  - Obenai the Visionary
  - Claudini the Builder
  - Cio the Day Dreamer
  - Gwoky the Timer Keeper
  - Mei the Force
- **AND** each portrait SHALL use a relative path to the `image/Team Members/` directory.
